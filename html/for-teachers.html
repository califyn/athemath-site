For teachers

We're looking for teachers who:
  • are of an underrepresented gender.
  • have qualified for the USA(J)MO or have won an Honorable Mention or above at the Math Prize for Girls or are at that level or have done a significant amount of math research, programming, etc., a strong math background.
  • want to teach younger students, but don't know where to start—or have taught for years, and want to take advantage of a great opportunity to scale up your teaching.
  • want to work with and get to know other young women who are interested in math!
In return, Athemath can offer you the following:
  • a source of ambitious students.
  • flexibility for your curriculum and topics.
  • structure for your course.
  • teaching without a high time commitment (1 instruction hr./2 weeks).
  • drawing tablet and a whiteboard subscription, on us.
  • an opportunity to be part of something bigger, and to help other girls doing math.

Teachers are paid for their work. Learn more about how our courses work here.
