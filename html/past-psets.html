Past Sets

Applications to be a new student for the Fall 2025 semester have closed, but applications to be a new student in Spring 2026 will open in December!

In order to apply to be a new student, we ask that you try your best at a problem set containing around five questions which range in difficulty from the AMC to the early Olympiad level. This will allow us to ensure that our students will benefit from the math aspect of Athemath, and will help us decide which class they will fit into. Historically, applicants who solved at least 1-2 problems have been admitted, however solving more can allow for placement into a harder class. Past problem sets can be found here.
